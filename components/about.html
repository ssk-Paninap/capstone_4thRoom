About 4thRoom

The product of the study is a web application named 4thRoom: Web Application School Decision Support Chatbot through the utilization of Natural Language Processing. The web application is a mobile-first platform that provides guidance and walkthroughs for students and visitors navigating various processes within Arellano University Andres Bonifacio Campus. These processes include Enrollment, Clearance, Discount processes, and more. Additionally, 4thRoom offers Announcement notices for campus events such as Fumigation, Orientation, Concerts, Intramurals, and Holidays, ensuring that users can plan ahead with ease upon deciding to visit the campus.

Features

Location Guidance

Provides precise locations and maps to navigate through the campus efficiently.

Step-by-Step Guidance

Offers detailed instructions for processes like enrollment, clearance, and discounts.

Event Announcements

Notifies users about upcoming campus events, ensuring they stay informed and prepared.
