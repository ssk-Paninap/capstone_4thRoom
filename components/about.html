About 4thRoom


The product of the study is a web application named 4thRoom: Web Application School Decision Support Chatbot through the utilization of Natural Language Processing. The web application will be a mobile first platform where the it provides guidance and walkthrough for students and visitors that have a certain process that they need to attend within Arellano University Andres Bonifacio Campus, the processes stated involves providing location, step by step guidance on what to do (e.g Enrollment process, Clearance, Discount processes etc.) and Announcement notices if ever there is an event within the campus (e.g. Fumigation, Orientation, Concert, Intramurals, Holidays etc.) ensuring that the users will be able to plan ahead with ease upon deciding to go to the campus.
